The Problem

When we read a good book we often wish to find out what happened next.

And often the authors have answered these questions in another book. However, these sequels can be obscure or out of print or hard to find. Many of us have taken a book home to enjoy, only to find that the rest of the story was out of print, and available in the public library or a used book fair if we were lucky. Robert Louis Stevenson's Kidnapped is far more famous (and easier to find) than its sequel Catriona. The Princess and the Goblin is a wonderful and famous story, but that story is completed in the lesser-known work, The Princess and Curdie.

Your search is over: we stock the whole story!

At The Whole Story, we stock sequels and prequels to your favorite works. You can buy omnibus editions as well as boxed sets, or just buy the stories in a series to read one by one! Our promise to you is that if we have one book in the series, we will have the next book in the series.
